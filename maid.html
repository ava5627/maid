So the explanation requires talking about the source material, but I’ll try to keep it as spoiler free as possible.

A Certain Magical Index is actually a spin off of the original A Certain Scientific Railgun manga about Misaka, and that’s where the level upper plot line came from.

The manga was originally only a short three chapter mini series but it got insanely popular so it got a full serialisation. Two side characters, Kamijou Touma and Kobayashi Maki, were particularly popular despite only showing up a few times.

They eventually got their own spin off which was set to be released around the same time the climax of the Level Upper arc would release. To drum up hype, they decided to do a crossover intersecting the climax of both series’ arcs.

At the time, it went fairly well, since most people reading Index were already up to date on Railgun.

When an anime adaptation was announced, people thought J.C.Staff would do the sensible thing and adapt both anime simultaneously, or maybe even combine them into one. Instead of doing that, they, for some godforsaken reason, decided to adapt Index a full year before adapting Railgun.

I fear the watch order discourse this will create.

TL;DR: Read the railgun manga or wait a year for the anime
